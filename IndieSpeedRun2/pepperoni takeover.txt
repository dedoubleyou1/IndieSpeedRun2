PROS of takeover:
-new product opportunities
-decreased competition :D


CONS of takeover:
-loss in goodwill (reputation co. enjoys with clients)
-unhappy employees -> slower production
-cost of purchase



DEFENSES against takeover:
-Pac-Man (turn the tables by attempting to buy the buyer!)
-crown jewel defense (sell off assets to become less attractive)
-golden parachute (excessive CEO severance packages)
